Frequently Asked Questions:




Can I alter or cancel my order?



While you can't change the details of an order once it's been placed, you can cancel it by contacting our customer support team.




Can I return a book in exchange for a refund?


As long as the book is returned within a month of the order and is in perfect condition, we are happy to offer refunds. Contact us via our customer support page.




My order has not arrived yet, what do I do?


We promise a level of consistency and as such if your order has not arrived within the time alloted in your email receipt, contact customer support and we will investigate. You are always entitled to a refund if the order is 7 days late.




Are the book prices at all varied between the Sweetshelf online store and bookshops?


Our shops' stock and prices are set independently. Our online book prices are subject to change but are only applied at the moment of an order.
