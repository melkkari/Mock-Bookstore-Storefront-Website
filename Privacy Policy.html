Privacy Policy:




Intention of this policy


This privacy policy outlines our approach to any of your personal information we collect from you or a third party. It describes where we process your personal information. This policy also describes your rights in respect to our processing of your personal information.




Confidentiality & Security


We respect the privacy of every person who visits or registers online or uses the services that we make available over the telephone) our customer support service, free in-store wi-fi service and loyalty plans. We strive to ensure a safe online experience. We strive to keep any private information provided to us safe and free from tampering. Our security policies, rules and technical measures are intended to protect your personal information from:

  • Unlawful damage or unintentional loss.
  • Unauthorised modification;
  • Improper use or divulgence;
  • Unapproved access;
All of our employees who have access to processing of personal information are required to respect the concealment of the personal information of all users.




How long do we store your details?


We will keep your data purely within legal guidelines and for the purposes mentioned in this privacy policy. We will never keep your personal information for longer than is necessary.
